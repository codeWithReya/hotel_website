About Kerala

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country", is one of the most beautiful states in India. Located on the southwestern coast, Kerala is famous for its peaceful backwaters, lush green landscapes, tropical beaches and rich cultural heritage. Visitors from around the world come here to experience its natural beauty, relaxing houseboat cruises and traditional Ayurvedic wellness.

Popular Places to Visit

Kerala offers many breathtaking destinations including the backwaters of Alleppey, the hill stations of Munnar, the beaches of Kovalam and the wildlife of Periyar National Park. These places provide a perfect blend of adventure, relaxation and natural beauty for travelers.

Famous Food in Kerala

Kerala cuisine is known for its rich flavors, coconut-based dishes and aromatic spices. The food reflects the coastal culture of the state and offers a unique culinary experience for travelers.
