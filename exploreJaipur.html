🏜 Explore Jaipur

About Jaipur

Jaipur, popularly known as the Pink City, is the capital of Rajasthan and one of India's most culturally rich travel destinations. The city is famous for its magnificent forts, royal palaces, colorful bazaars and vibrant Rajasthani culture. Visitors come to Jaipur to experience its royal heritage, beautiful architecture and traditional hospitality.

Popular Places to Visit

Jaipur is home to several iconic landmarks including the majestic Amber Fort, the beautiful Hawa Mahal, the royal City Palace and the astronomical wonder Jantar Mantar. These places showcase the architectural brilliance and historical richness of Rajasthan.

Famous Rajasthani Food

Jaipur is also famous for its traditional Rajasthani cuisine. The food is rich in flavors and spices and reflects the royal heritage of Rajasthan. Travelers must try these iconic dishes.
